# Coffee Brew Parameters Reference

Quick lookup for common brew methods, ratios, temps, and grind ranges.

---

## Ratios (coffee : total liquid)

| Method | Ratio | Typical Dose | Total Water |
|--------|-------|--------------|-------------|
| Flash brew (Japanese iced) | 1:13 – 1:15 | 30–35 g | 400–500 g (half ice) |
| Hot pour-over (V60) | 1:15 – 1:17 | 20–25 g | 300–425 g |
| Hot pour-over (flat-bottom) | 1:15 – 1:17 | 30–35 g | 450–600 g |
| AeroPress (standard) | 1:13 – 1:15 | 15–18 g | 200–250 g |
| AeroPress (inverted) | 1:12 – 1:14 | 18–20 g | 220–260 g |
| French Press | 1:15 – 1:17 | 30–40 g | 450–680 g |
| Cold brew (concentrate) | 1:5 – 1:8 | 100–200 g | 500–1000 g |

---

## Water Temperature by Roast

| Roast Level | Temp (°F) | Temp (°C) | Notes |
|-------------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Light (Nordic) | 208–212 | 98–100 | Max extraction for density |
| Light-Medium | 205–208 | 96–98 | Sweet spot for most Ethiopians/Kenyans |
| Medium | 203–205 | 95–96 | Balanced |
| Medium-Dark | 200–203 | 93–95 | Avoid over-extraction |
| Dark | 195–200 | 90–93 | Low density, extracts fast |

**Fellow Stagg EKG:** Use Hold mode. Set 205°F for light-medium (this recipe).

## Grind Reference (1Zpresso K-Ultra clicks)

*Click counts are approximate — calibrate to your burrs.*

| Method | Clicks | Feel | Comparison |
|--------|--------|------|------------|
| Espresso | 8–12 | Fine powder | Table salt |
| AeroPress (fine) | 14–18 | Fine | Slightly coarser than espresso |
| V60 02 (conical) | 26–28 | Medium-fine | Kosher salt |
| Blue Bottle / Kalita (flat) | 23–25 | Medium | Sea salt |
| Chemex | 28–32 | Medium-coarse | Rough sea salt |
| French Press | 34–38 | Coarse | Coarse sea salt |
| Cold Brew | 40+ | Very coarse | Peppercorns |

**Flash brew adjustment:** Same grind as hot equivalent, or 1–2 clicks finer (less contact time).

---

## Flash Brew Specifics

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Ice : Hot Water | 1:1 by weight (standard) |
| Bloom ratio | 2–3× coffee weight |
| Bloom time | 30–50 sec |
| Total brew time (hot phase) | 2:00–2:45 |
| Target drawdown finish | 2:15–2:45 (flat-bottom), 1:45–2:15 (V60) |

## Common Coffee Profiles

| Origin/Process | Typical Notes | Brew Adjustments |
|----------------|---------------|------------------|
| Ethiopia Washed | Floral, tea-like, citrus | 205°F, standard ratio, don't over-extract |
| Ethiopia Natural | Berry, winey, heavy body | 203°F, slightly coarser, shorter time |
| **Ethiopia Honey** | **Floral + sweet, sticky body** | **205°F, if muddy → coarser** |
| Kenya Washed | Bright acidity, blackcurrant | 208°F, finer grind, fast pour |
| Colombia Washed | Caramel, nut, balanced | 205°F, standard |
| Brazil Natural | Chocolate, nut, low acid | 203°F, coarser, longer bloom |
| Panama Gesha | Floral, tea, delicate | 205–208°F, very precise, low agitation |
